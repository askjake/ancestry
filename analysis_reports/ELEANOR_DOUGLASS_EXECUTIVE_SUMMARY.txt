• Ancient Scottish clan from Lanarkshire (Glasgow area)
• Name derived from "Dubh glas" (Gaelic for "dark stream")
• Major Scottish noble family, fought in Wars of Independence
• Famous members: Sir James Douglas ("Good Sir James"), Black Douglas
• Geographic base: Scottish Lowlands (not Highlands)

AMERICAN IMMIGRATION:
• Douglass/Douglas families immigrated to America 1600s-1900s
• Major settlements: Pennsylvania, Virginia, Carolinas, Georgia
