PART 1: FAMILYSEARCH.ORG EXACT SEARCH INSTRUCTIONS
================================================================================

STEP 1: CREATE FREE ACCOUNT
----------------------------
1. Go to: https://www.familysearch.org
2. Click "Free Account" (top right)
3. Enter email, create password
4. Verify email
5. Login

STEP 2: CALIFORNIA BIRTH INDEX SEARCH
--------------------------------------
1. After login, click "Search" in top menu
2. Click "Records"
3. In search bar, type: "California Birth Index"
4. Click on: "California Birth Index, 1905-1995"
   Direct link: https://www.familysearch.org/search/collection/2001287

SEARCH PARAMETERS TO ENTER:

Search #1: Primary spelling
  First Names: Eleanor
  Last Names: Douglas
  Birth Year: 1925
  Birth Year Range: +/- 2 years
  Birth Place: California, United States
  [Click SEARCH]

Search #2: Alternate spelling
  First Names: Eleanor
  Last Names: Douglass
  Birth Year: 1925
  Birth Year Range: +/- 2 years
  Birth Place: California, United States
  [Click SEARCH]

Search #3: Broader search
  First Names: Eleanor Elenore
  Last Names: Douglas Douglass
  Birth Year: 1924-1926
  Birth Place: California, United States
  [Click SEARCH]

Search #4: Nickname variations
  First Names: Lenore Nora Nell
  Last Names: Douglas Douglass
  Birth Year: 1924-1926
  Birth Place: California, United States
  [Click SEARCH]

WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN RESULTS:
  ✓ Name matches Eleanor (or variant)
  ✓ Birth year 1924-1926
  ✓ County in California
  ⭐ MOTHER'S MAIDEN NAME field - THIS IS KEY!
  ✓ Certificate number (for ordering official copy)

IF YOU FIND A MATCH:
  1. Click on the record to view details
  2. Screenshot or save the information
  3. Note: Name, Date, County, Mother's maiden name, Certificate #
  4. Click "Save to My List" or "Add to Family Tree"
  5. Order official certificate using certificate number

STEP 3: 1940 US CENSUS SEARCH
------------------------------
1. From main FamilySearch page, click "Search" → "Records"
2. Type: "1940 United States Census"
3. Click on collection
   Direct link: https://www.familysearch.org/search/collection/2000219

SEARCH PARAMETERS:

Search #1:
  Name: Eleanor Douglas
  Birth Year: 1925 (+/- 2 years)
  Birth Place: California
  Residence: California
  [Click SEARCH]

Search #2:
  Name: Eleanor Douglass
  Birth Year: 1925 (+/- 2 years)
  Birth Place: California
  Residence: California
  [Click SEARCH]

WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN RESULTS:
  ✓ Eleanor listed with age 14-16 in 1940
  ✓ Living in California household
  ✓ PARENTS listed in same household (names!)
  ✓ Siblings listed
  ✓ Address and city
  ✓ Father's occupation
  ✓ Click on record to see full household image

IF YOU FIND THE HOUSEHOLD:
  1. View the actual census page image
  2. Find Eleanor's line
  3. Look at head of household (likely father)
  4. Look at wife of head (likely mother)
  5. Note all names and relationships
  6. Screenshot the census page
  7. This gives you PARENTS' NAMES!

STEP 4: CALIFORNIA MARRIAGE INDEX SEARCH
-----------------------------------------
1. From FamilySearch, search for "California County Marriages"
2. Collection: California, County Marriages, 1850-1952
   Direct link: https://www.familysearch.org/search/collection/1804002

SEARCH PARAMETERS:

Search for Eleanor as BRIDE:
  Bride's Given Name: Eleanor
  Bride's Surname: Douglas OR Douglass
  Groom's Given Name: Jack
  Groom's Surname: Wilson
  Marriage Year: 1945-1953
  Marriage Place: California
  [Click SEARCH]

WHAT TO LOOK FOR:
  ✓ Marriage record Eleanor Douglass to Jack Wilson
  ✓ Marriage date and county
  ✓ Parents' names for BOTH bride and groom
  ⭐ This will list Eleanor's father and mother!

PART 4: DETAILED SEARCH STRATEGIES
================================================================================

STRATEGY 1: NAME VARIATIONS

Eleanor variations to try:
- Eleanor
- Elenore  
- Eleanore
- Elenor
- Lenore (nickname)
- Nora (nickname)
- Nell (nickname)
- Nellie (nickname)
- Ellen (related name)

Douglas variations to try:
- Douglas
- Douglass
- Duglas
- Dougles

STRATEGY 2: YEAR VARIATIONS

Try each year individually:
- 1923 (if birth year estimate off)
- 1924
- 1925
- 1926
- 1927 (if birth year estimate off)

STRATEGY 3: COUNTY VARIATIONS

California counties to search in order of priority:
1. Los Angeles County (highest population)
2. Ventura County (daughter born here)
3. Orange County
4. San Bernardino County
5. Kern County
6. San Diego County
7. Riverside County
8. Santa Barbara County

STRATEGY 4: SOUNDEX SEARCH

Some databases have "Soundex" or "Similar Names" option
This finds phonetically similar names:
- Douglas = Douglass = Duglas
Enable this option when available

STRATEGY 5: WILDCARD SEARCH

Some databases allow wildcards:
- Dougla* (finds Douglas, Douglass, etc.)
- Ele*nor (finds Eleanor, Elenor, etc.)

STRATEGY 6: BROWSE MODE

If search not working:
- Browse California Birth Index by year
- Look through all births in target county for 1925
- Tedious but thorough

PART 5: CROSS-REFERENCE VERIFICATION
================================================================================

Once you find a potential match, VERIFY by cross-referencing:

CHECKLIST FOR VERIFICATION:
□ Name matches: Eleanor Douglass/Douglas
□ Birth year range: 1924-1926
□ Birth location: California
□ Marriage to: Jack Warren Wilson (verify separately)
□ Daughter: Melinda Lee Wilson born 1953 Simi Valley
□ Parents' names make sense (age appropriate, etc.)
□ No conflicting information in other records

MULTIPLE SOURCES NEEDED:
✓ Birth record (parents' names)
✓ Census record (confirm family household)
✓ Marriage record (confirm Eleanor + Jack)
✓ Daughter's birth record (confirms Eleanor as mother)

If all 4 sources align = HIGH CONFIDENCE
If 2-3 sources align = MEDIUM CONFIDENCE  
If only 1 source = NEEDS MORE VERIFICATION

PART 6: TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

IF YOU DON'T FIND ANYTHING:

Problem: No matches in California Birth Index
Solutions:
→ Try all name variations
→ Try adjacent years (1923, 1927)
→ Try misspellings (Elonor, Dougles, etc.)
→ Check if born in different state
→ Birth may not have been registered (rare but possible)

Problem: Multiple matches found
Solutions:
→ Check each one for age/location match
→ Look for census records to verify household
→ Check marriage records to confirm spouse
→ Look for daughter's birth record naming mother

Problem: Record found but no parents listed
Solutions:
→ Order official certificate (has more detail than index)
→ Check census records instead
→ Look for delayed birth registration (filed later)
→ Check marriage record for parents

Problem: Can't access FamilySearch/Ancestry
Solutions:
→ Visit local library with Ancestry Library Edition
→ Contact Simi Valley FamilySearch Center for help
→ Try free MyHeritage.com trial
→ Check WikiTree.com for user-submitted trees

PART 7: WHEN TO HIRE A PROFESSIONAL
================================================================================

Consider hiring a professional genealogist if:
- You've searched for 3+ months with no results
- You've exhausted all online databases
- Records may require in-person archive research
- You're stuck and frustrated

CALIFORNIA GENEALOGIST RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Association of Professional Genealogists: www.apgen.org
- Board for Certification of Genealogists: www.bcgcertification.org
- Filter by: California specialization
- Expected cost: $50-$100/hour
- Typical project: 5-10 hours for focused research
- Total cost: $250-$1000

WHAT A PROFESSIONAL CAN DO:
✓ Access paid databases you don't have
✓ Visit California State Archives in person
✓ Request records from multiple counties
✓ Read old handwriting in original documents
✓ Know which resources to check
✓ Often find records in 1-2 weeks that you couldn't find in months

PART 8: RECORDING YOUR FINDINGS
================================================================================

USE THE DAILY JOURNAL TEMPLATE PROVIDED

1. Source (database name, collection, URL)
2. Exact search terms used
3. What you found
4. Citation information
5. Screenshot or save copy
6. Date you found it
7. Confidence level (high/medium/low)

BUILD SOURCE CITATIONS:
Example birth record citation:
"Eleanor Douglass, birth record, 15 March 1925, Los Angeles County, 
California; California Birth Index, 1905-1995; FamilySearch 
(https://www.familysearch.org : accessed 12 March 2026), 
certificate number 1925-012345."

This allows you (or others) to find the same record later.
